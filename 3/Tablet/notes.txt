 1/RPM * 24 = s/blk
Deployer:
 1/RPM * 48 = s/updwn
Fan:
 1/RPM * 15 = s/blk
Mechanical Piston:
  1/RPM * 24 = s/blk
Mill:
  1/32 * 204 = s/blk

  1/RPM * 24 = s/blk
Pump:
  1/RPM * 96 = s/blk
  * 2 if not from container
Saw (Chop):
  1/RPM * 96 = s/blk
Saw (Cut):
  1/RPM * 32 = s/blk_o
